Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an interactive Dungeons & Dragons player stat sheet that allows users to input and track their character’s stats and abilities for gameplay.

Audience

This site is designed for Dungeons & Dragons players who need a digital, customizable, and interactive way to manage and track their character’s stats and abilities throughout their campaign.

Dynamic elements

Using JavaScript, this site allows players to input and modify their character’s stats, calculate modifiers automatically, track hit points and armor class, and adjust skills based on their character’s class, race, and level. The site will save player data in the browser’s localStorage, allowing it to persist across sessions.
